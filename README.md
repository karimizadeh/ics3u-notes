These are the lesson notes from Ms. Marie for Grade 11 Computer Science. These lessons are an introduction to programming and Python, and require no prior skills or experience in programming.

Throughout the lessons there may be links labeled “Practice #0” or similar. Clicking on them will bring you to a CodeCheck practice assignment with instructions. To complete them, just put in the code required to solve the problem presented in the white boxes and press “CodeCheck” at the bottom to verify your code.
